Inheritance

1.	Its a mechanism of consuming members of one class by another class by establishing parent/child relationship 
	between the involved classes.
2.	A child class can consume all members of its parent class as if it owns the members except the 
	private members of the class.
3.  For this to work. The parent aclass constructor must be accessible to child class.
4.	Parent class can never acess the variables of the child class.

ex check Inhreritance1 as parent aclass and Inheritance1Child as Child Class
